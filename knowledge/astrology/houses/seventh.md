# The Seventh House

## Header
**Cusp:** the Descendant (the western horizon, where the Sun sets). **Angular.** **Natural sign:** Libra. **Natural ruler:** Venus. **Domain:** partnerships, marriage, the open enemy, contracts, the other.

## Core Meaning
The room of the encounter. The seventh house begins at the western horizon, opposite the Ascendant — the place where the figure meets the other. Read as the partner, the spouse, the close adversary, the contract — every relationship in which the figure becomes a "we" rather than an "I."

## Upright
Tradition reads the seventh as the house of marriage and of the open enemy — the person across the table, in either friendly or hostile encounter. Planets here describe the qualities sought in partners and the dynamics that show up in close one-to-one relationships. Venus here favours partnership. Saturn here gives serious partners, often delayed. Mars here brings conflict into the relationship, sometimes constructively. The Sun here gives identity through union.

## In a Chart
The seventh house ruler's condition shows the quality of partnership life and what tends to be encountered in others. The Descendant is often read as the qualities the figure has projected outside — the partner is the carrier of what the figure has not yet integrated. The first-seventh axis is the self-other axis: what the body knows about itself (first) becomes negotiable through encounter (seventh).

## Keywords
the other, partnership, marriage, the Descendant, the contract, the open enemy, the encounter, projection
