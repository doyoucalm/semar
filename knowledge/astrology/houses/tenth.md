# The Tenth House

## Header
**Cusp:** the Midheaven (MC — Medium Coeli, the highest point of the chart). **Angular.** **Natural sign:** Capricorn. **Natural ruler:** Saturn. **Domain:** career, public reputation, the mother (in traditional reading) or the public-facing parent, authority, the visible life.

## Core Meaning
The crown of the chart. The tenth house begins at the highest point of the sky at the moment of birth — the noon position, the most visible place. Read as the figure's public face, the work that meets the world, the contribution recognised in the eyes of others.

## Upright
Tradition reads the tenth as the house of the mother (in some lineages) and of the king — the authority figure, the public position. Modern practice reads it as career and public reputation. Planets here are highly visible. The Sun in the tenth makes the identity public. Saturn in the tenth often gives a serious, slowly-built career. Jupiter in the tenth often gives a fortunate public position. Mars in the tenth fights for public standing.

## In a Chart
The tenth house ruler's condition shows the quality and shape of the public life. The fourth-tenth axis is the foundation-crown axis: a tenth without a fourth becomes a hollow public life; a fourth without a tenth becomes a private life that has not yet contributed. The MC is one of the chart's four major points — the planets near it shape what the chart is publicly for.

## Keywords
career, the public face, the MC, the crown, reputation, authority, the visible life, the contribution
