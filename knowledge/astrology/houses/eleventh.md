# The Eleventh House

## Header
**Succedent.** **Natural sign:** Aquarius. **Natural ruler:** Saturn (traditional), Uranus (modern). **Domain:** friends, groups, networks, hopes and wishes, the larger collective.

## Core Meaning
The room of the chosen company. The eleventh house holds the friendships and movements the figure joins — not by blood (fourth) or by intimate contract (seventh), but by affinity. Read as the network, the cause, the community where the figure's hopes are aimed.

## Upright
Tradition reads the eleventh as the house of friends, of patrons, and of "good spirit" (the Hellenistic name agathos daimōn). Planets here describe the texture of friendships and the kinds of groups the figure joins. Jupiter here often gives many helpful friends. Venus here gives warm friendships and aesthetic communities. Saturn here gives few but enduring friends, and sometimes the work of organising groups. Uranus here gives the unconventional company.

## In a Chart
The eleventh house ruler's condition shows the quality of the network and the realisation of long-term hopes. The fifth-eleventh axis is the creative-collective axis: what is made privately for joy (fifth) and what is contributed to the wider community (eleventh). The eleventh is also the house of patronage in classical sources — the people whose support enables the figure's work.

## Keywords
friends, the network, the chosen company, groups, hopes, the collective, patronage, the wider community
