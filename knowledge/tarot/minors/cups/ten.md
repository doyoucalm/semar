# Ten of Cups

## Image
A man and woman stand with arms around each other's waists; two children dance beside them. Above, ten cups arc as a rainbow. Behind, a small house and green hills.

## Core Meaning
The completion of the family. Where the Two is the vow between two and the Three is the celebration of community, the Ten is the home built and the family alive within it. Read as domestic happiness, the lineage flowing, the long satisfaction of belonging.

## Upright
Tradition reads it as the home, the marriage that has matured into family, the village of belonging. The rainbow is the suit's brightest sign — the long water of feeling resolved into its full spectrum of relationship. The dancing children matter: the joy is not contained in the couple alone.

## Reversed
The home cracked, or the image of family that no longer matches the reality. Sometimes the question of whether the conventional happy family is actually the questioner's path — the rainbow that turns out to be someone else's picture.

## Keywords
domestic happiness, family complete, the rainbow, belonging, the lineage, home, false image (reversed), domestic strain (reversed)
