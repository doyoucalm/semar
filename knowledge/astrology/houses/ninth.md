# The Ninth House

## Header
**Cadent.** **Natural sign:** Sagittarius. **Natural ruler:** Jupiter. **Domain:** higher mind, philosophy, religion, long journeys, foreign cultures, higher education, the search for meaning.

## Core Meaning
The room of the long view. The ninth house holds the journeys that take the figure out of their native country — geographic, intellectual, or spiritual. Read as the search for meaning, the philosophy that organises a life, the foreign teacher, the framework large enough to make sense of the small particulars.

## Upright
Tradition reads the ninth as the house of religion, of long-distance travel, of higher study, and of vision (in the prophetic sense — the planet of the ninth was once called the "joy" of the Sun). Planets here describe the figure's relationship to meaning. Jupiter here is at home, giving a natural philosopher. Saturn here gives a serious student of one or two large systems. The Sun here gives an identity organised around the search for truth.

## In a Chart
The ninth house ruler's condition shows the quality of the figure's intellectual and spiritual life. The third-ninth axis is the local-mind / long-view-mind axis: what is learned daily (third) becomes synthesised into framework (ninth). Modern practice reads publishing, teaching at higher levels, and long-distance travel as ninth-house activities.

## Keywords
the long view, philosophy, religion, the foreign country, higher study, the search for meaning, the long journey, vision
