# Nine of Cups

## Image
A round, satisfied figure sits on a wooden bench with arms crossed. Nine cups are arranged in an arc on a draped table behind him. He smiles.

## Core Meaning
The wish fulfilled. The card is known traditionally as the "wish card" — the cups are arranged like trophies, the figure has gotten what he wanted. Read as satisfaction, the well-fed pleasure of having arrived.

## Upright
Tradition reads it as the literal fulfilment of a wish, the moment of contented success, the dinner one has paid for and earned. There is some self-satisfaction in the figure's posture — and the card sometimes warns against complacency — but the joy is real.

## Reversed
The wish granted without the joy expected; or the satisfaction that has tipped into smugness. Sometimes the wish was the wrong wish and now the figure cannot say so.

## Keywords
wish fulfilled, contented success, the well-fed, satisfaction, smugness (reversed), the wrong wish (reversed)
