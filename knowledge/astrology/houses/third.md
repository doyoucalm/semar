# The Third House

## Header
**Cadent.** **Natural sign:** Gemini. **Natural ruler:** Mercury. **Domain:** communication, siblings, neighbours, short trips, the immediate environment, early schooling.

## Core Meaning
The room of the local. The third house is the territory the figure crosses every day — the streets, the relatives, the messages, the conversations that make up a life. Read as the daily mind in motion, the texture of how the figure thinks and exchanges in the everyday.

## Upright
Tradition reads the third house as siblings, neighbours, short journeys, and the speaking voice. Planets here describe the texture of speech and the quality of close-range relationships. Mercury here is its own house, sharpening the mind. Venus here makes the voice pleasant and the neighbourhood warm. Mars here makes the speech direct, sometimes argumentative. Saturn here often gives a careful, perhaps inhibited, communicator.

## In a Chart
The third house ruler's condition shows how the figure communicates and what their early environment was like. Hellenistic tradition reads the third as the house of the "goddess" — perhaps a remnant of religious learning, or a connection to ritual and minor sacred practice. Modern practice often reads the third as schooling and the early mind, with the ninth house as its complement (higher mind, philosophical study).
