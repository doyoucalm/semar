# The Eighth House

## Header
**Succedent.** **Natural sign:** Scorpio. **Natural ruler:** Mars (traditional), Pluto (modern). **Domain:** death, other people's resources, inheritance, intimacy, transformation, the occult.

## Core Meaning
The room of the shared depths. The eighth house holds what is exchanged in intimacy, what is inherited from the dead, and what passes through the underworld and returns. Read as the part of the chart that deals with what is not the figure's alone — partner's money, inherited estates, taxes, the long descent into psychological depth.

## Upright
Tradition reads the eighth as the house of death, of the partner's money, of inheritance, and of what is hidden. Modern practice expands the reading to include psychological transformation, sexual intimacy, and the occult. Planets here describe what the figure does in the shared depths. Pluto here makes the work transformative. Venus here gives passionate intimate life. Saturn here often gives long, slow inheritance work — sometimes psychological, sometimes literal.

## In a Chart
The eighth house ruler's condition shows the quality of the figure's relationship to depth and to shared resources. The second-eighth axis is the personal-shared resource axis: what the figure earns (second) and what flows through the figure from others (eighth). The eighth is also the house of the death of the ego — the underworld passages where the old self is unmade and the new one slowly returns.

## Keywords
the depths, intimacy, the partner's resources, inheritance, transformation, the occult, death and rebirth, the underworld
