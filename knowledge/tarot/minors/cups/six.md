# Six of Cups

## Image
A boy in old-fashioned clothes hands a cup full of white flowers to a smaller girl. Five more flowered cups are arranged in the courtyard. A figure in the background walks away.

## Core Meaning
Nostalgia, childhood, the return to an older form of feeling. Read as memory, the visit of a person from earlier in one's life, the kindness offered between younger and older.

## Upright
Tradition reads it as the return to one's past — the hometown, the family, the old friend, the smell that triggers a memory. Innocence, kindness, simplicity. The card can also mean a literal child appearing, or the recovery of one's own childhood capacities.

## Reversed
Stuck in nostalgia — the inability to leave the past, the over-romanticised childhood that does not match what was actually lived. Sometimes the return is the obligation rather than the joy.

## Keywords
nostalgia, childhood, return, the kindness between ages, memory, the past, stuck in nostalgia (reversed)
