# Scorpio (♏)

## Glyph
The scorpion's body with a barbed tail rising. The M with an arrowed strike — the venom held at the end, the depths giving warning.

## Header
**Element:** Water. **Modality:** Fixed. **Traditional ruler:** Mars. **Modern ruler:** Pluto. **Exaltation:** none (traditional) / Uranus (some moderns). **Detriment:** Venus. **Fall:** Moon. **Body:** Reproductive organs, the eliminative system. **Mythic figure:** the scorpion that killed Orion; Persephone returning from the underworld; the phoenix.

## Core Meaning
The depths. Scorpio is the eighth sign, the dark half of the year beginning, the moment when the leaves rot and the seed goes underground. Read as intensity, transformation, the figure willing to look at what most people refuse to see.

## Strength
Tradition reads Scorpio as the investigator, the healer of trauma, the lover whose intimacy demands the truth, the figure who knows where the bodies are buried. The fixed water is depth held over time: Scorpio placements do not skim. The sign loves what is hidden — secrets, taboos, the underside — and dignifies it by paying attention. Mars's traditional rulership gives Scorpio a warrior's resolve; Pluto's modern overlay gives it a relationship with death and renewal.

## Shadow
The same depth turned to obsession or revenge. Scorpio placements may keep their grievances polished for years, may control through silence, may use intimacy as leverage. The shadow is the figure who has confused power with love, the scorpion who stings the one who carries it across the river.

## In a Chart
The house holding Scorpio shows where the chart goes deep — where the figure refuses surface, where transformation happens, where intimacy and money and other people's resources become the work. Mars's condition (and Pluto's, for modern readers) colours how that depth moves: a dignified Mars makes the Scorpio placement courageous; an afflicted Mars makes it vindictive. The Moon in Scorpio is in fall — the emotional body holds more than it can comfortably carry.

## Keywords
the depths, fixed water, transformation, intensity, the investigator, intimacy, taboo, obsession (shadow), control (shadow), vengeance (shadow)
