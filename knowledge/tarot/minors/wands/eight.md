# Eight of Wands

## Image
Eight wands fly through a clear sky in parallel formation, headed downward, about to land. A pastoral landscape with a river is below. No human figure appears.

## Core Meaning
Swift movement. The card depicts arrows in flight — messages, momentum, the chapter of fast events between sending and arrival. Read as speed, news, the rapid progression of an unfolding situation.

## Upright
This is the suit's pure-velocity card. Tradition reads it as travel, communication arriving fast, the project that suddenly accelerates after a slow period. There is no rest implied; the wands are still in the air. The questioner's task is often to keep up rather than to direct.

## Reversed
Speed turned chaotic — too many messages, conflicting signals, the trajectory off-course. Sometimes the delay is the meaning: the wands have not landed yet and impatience is rising.

## Keywords
speed, momentum, swift news, travel, rapid progression, the wands in flight, chaos (reversed), delay (reversed)
