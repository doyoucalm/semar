# Six Combinations (六合)

## Header
**Type:** branch combination (pair). **Pairs:** 子丑 / 寅亥 / 卯戌 / 辰酉 / 巳申 / 午未. **Effect:** mutual attraction; the two branches turn toward each other and may form a new element.

## Core Meaning
The pairs that attract. Six Combinations are the twelve earthly branches arranged as six pairs, each pair drawn together as if magnetised. Read as the relationships that pull two parts of the chart into agreement — sometimes productively, sometimes by softening what should have stayed distinct.

## The Six Pairs

| Pair | Combined Element | Note |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 子 + 丑 | Earth (sometimes Water in some lineages) | The water-rat meets the earth-ox; tradition is split. |
| 寅 + 亥 | Wood | The tiger and the pig combine into wood. |
| 卯 + 戌 | Fire | The rabbit and the dog combine into fire. |
| 辰 + 酉 | Metal | The dragon and the rooster combine into metal. |
| 巳 + 申 | Water (sometimes Metal) | The snake and the monkey; the combination is fragile because of underlying clash. |
| 午 + 未 | Fire (or Earth, lineage-dependent) | The horse and the goat — the southern combination. |

## In a Chart
A six-combination between two branches shows places in the chart where two themes naturally cooperate. The pair sometimes "binds" — the branches lose some of their independent function and act together. Whether the combination produces the new element depends on whether the chart provides the conditions: a 卯戌 combination only fully transforms into fire if the chart has supporting fire roots.

## When the Combination Helps
The combination is useful when the new element is the chart's needed element. A weak fire 丁 day master with 卯戌 in the chart that forms fire receives valuable support from the combination. Combinations between the year and month, or between the day and hour, suggest harmony in family origins (year-month) or in the inner life and outcomes (day-hour).

## When the Combination Hurts
The combination is unwelcome when it ties up a useful branch. A useful 酉 metal that is bound by 辰 may no longer act as the chart needs. Combinations involving 申巳 can be unstable — the underlying clash often re-emerges, especially in luck pillars or annual transits.

## Keywords
the pair, the attraction, mutual binding, the transformation, the harmony, the lost function (shadow when useful branch is bound)
