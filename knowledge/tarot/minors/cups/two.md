# Two of Cups

## Image
A man and a woman face each other, each holding a cup. Above them rises a caduceus topped with a winged lion's head — the alchemical symbol of union.

## Core Meaning
The vow between two. Romantic union, partnership, the recognition of like in like. Read as the card of true meeting — not infatuation but the deep nod of one soul to another.

## Upright
Tradition reads it as the marriage card of the minors, the close friendship, the business partnership built on mutual respect. The cups are equal; the meeting is even.

## Reversed
The meeting unbalanced — one cup empty, one full. Sometimes the bond that has lost its evenness; sometimes the partner refused for fear of what mutuality requires.

## Keywords
union, partnership, meeting of equals, the vow, mutual recognition, imbalance (reversed)
