# 正官 Direct Officer (Zheng Guan) — The Authority and the Standard

## Core Concept
Direct Officer (Zheng Guan) represents the element that controls the Day Master with the opposite polarity. In a traditional family, it represents the husband (for women) or the father (for men). In the world, it represents authority, law, social standing, and the moral standard one adheres to.

## Psychological Profile

**Archetype:** The judge, the diplomat, the honorable citizen, the administrator.

**Positive Traits:**
- **Integrity:** Zheng Guan people are fundamentally honest and value their reputation above all else. They are the "straight arrows" of society.
- **Responsibility:** They have a deep sense of duty. If they say they will do something, they do it.
- **Diplomacy:** They understand how to operate within systems and how to use "soft power" to achieve results.
- **Fairness:** They believe in the law and in treating everyone according to a consistent standard.

**Negative Traits (when excessive or unfavorable):**
- **Rigidity:** They can become so obsessed with the "rules" that they lose sight of the "spirit" of the law.
- **Pedantry:** They may focus on minor details of protocol at the expense of progress.
- **Fear of Failure:** Because their reputation is so important, they may be paralyzed by the fear of making a mistake.
- **Oppressive:** When too strong, Zheng Guan can feel like a heavy weight that crushes the individual's spirit (the "controlling" nature of the star).

## Practical Manifestations

**In Career:**
They thrive in structured environments: government, law, corporate management, and administration. They are the perfect "second-in-command" or the person who keeps an organization running smoothly. They respect hierarchy and expect others to do the same.

**In Relationship:**
They seek stability and social approval. For women, this star traditionally represents the husband — a source of protection and status. In modern terms, it represents a desire for a partner who is honorable, reliable, and respected by the community.

**Wealth Dynamics:**
Zheng Guan "protects" Wealth. While it doesn't create wealth directly, it provides the structure and the reputation that allow wealth to be maintained and grown over time. A person with a strong Direct Officer star is unlikely to lose money through impulsive or dishonorable actions.

## Symbolic Meaning
- **The Internal Compass:** The voice that tells us what is "right."
- **Social Order:** The rules that allow a society to function.
- **The Honorable Self:** The part of us that seeks to be a "good person" in the eyes of others.

## The Zheng Guan Question:
"What is the standard you live by? And does this standard empower you, or just restrict you?"
