# Peach Blossom (桃花)

## Header
**Category:** romance / attraction. **Anchor:** year or day branch. **Rule:** the year (or day) branch determines a single branch that, if present elsewhere in the chart, brings the Peach Blossom star.

## Lookup
| Year/Day branch | Peach Blossom |
| --- | --- |
| 申, 子, 辰 | 酉 |
| 亥, 卯, 未 | 子 |
| 寅, 午, 戌 | 卯 |
| 巳, 酉, 丑 | 午 |

The Peach Blossom is always the "cardinal" branch (子卯午酉) of the triad it answers to.

## Core Meaning
The star of charm and romantic magnetism. Peach Blossom represents the figure's attractiveness to others — physical, social, sexual. Read as the natural drawing power, the figure people gravitate toward, the chemistry of being noticed.

## In a Chart
A chart with Peach Blossom in any pillar brings romantic and social attention to the figure. The star's house position matters: in the year branch it brings attention from outside the family; in the month branch, from professional or social context; in the day branch, in marriage; in the hour branch, in later life or through children.

Classical tradition distinguishes **inner Peach Blossom** (within the year-month, before marriage) and **outer Peach Blossom** (in day-hour, indicating romance after marriage — sometimes read as susceptibility to affairs). Modern practice is less moralistic about this distinction.

## When Activated
The Peach Blossom is activated by transit. A 子-year encounter for a figure whose chart has 酉 as a Peach Blossom often brings romantic events that year — meetings, attractions, new relationships. The activation can also bring social attention more broadly: performance, public visibility, the year of being noticed.

## In Practice
Peach Blossom is one of the most popular stars in modern Chinese metaphysical reading because of its romantic association. Tradition does not always read it as flattering — too many Peach Blossom branches can give a figure who is so caught in their own charm or in others' attention that the deeper work of the chart stalls.

## Keywords
charm, romantic attraction, the magnetism, the noticed, the romance, the social presence, vanity (shadow), distraction (shadow)
