# Aquarius (♒)

## Glyph
The water bearer's stream — two horizontal waves. Often misread as the water itself; the older symbol shows the jar tipped, the water poured for the people.

## Header
**Element:** Air. **Modality:** Fixed. **Traditional ruler:** Saturn. **Modern ruler:** Uranus. **Detriment:** Sun. **Body:** Ankles, circulatory system, the nervous system's outer reaches. **Mythic figure:** Ganymede, the cup-bearer of Olympus; the figure who carries water to the polis.

## Core Meaning
The mind serving the collective. Aquarius is the eleventh sign, the late winter when the year prepares its renewal, the moment when the personal must reach toward the shared. Read as innovation, friendship, the figure whose work is for the many rather than the one.

## Strength
Tradition reads Aquarius as the inventor, the reformer, the friend, the figure whose vision is too wide for the household. The fixed air is committed thinking: Aquarius placements hold ideas the way Taurus holds land. Saturn's traditional rulership gives Aquarius the discipline to work on slow systemic problems; Uranus's modern overlay gives it the spark to see what others have not yet noticed. The sign loves the network, the movement, the principle that applies to everyone equally.

## Shadow
The same air turned distant. Aquarius placements may love humanity in the abstract and dislike the actual humans in front of them, may use ideology as a wall against intimacy, may insist on principles when what is wanted is presence. The shadow is the figure whose head has flown so high that the heart can no longer reach it.

## In a Chart
The house holding Aquarius shows where the chart works for the group — where friendships, networks, and collective projects live, where the figure thinks about systems rather than individuals. Saturn's condition (and Uranus's, for modern readers) colours the work: a dignified Saturn makes the Aquarius placement a true reformer; an afflicted Uranus makes it merely contrarian. The Sun in Aquarius is in detriment, suggesting an identity that finds itself only through the collective.

## Keywords
the collective, fixed air, innovation, friendship, the network, the reformer, principle, detachment (shadow), abstraction-over-presence (shadow)
