# The Second House

## Header
**Succedent.** **Natural sign:** Taurus. **Natural ruler:** Venus. **Domain:** money, possessions, values, the body's earnings.

## Core Meaning
What the figure holds in their hands. The second house is the room of personal resource — earned income, the things owned, the values that organise spending and keeping. Read as the relationship between the figure and material life: how they earn, how they spend, what they treat as worth.

## Upright
Tradition reads the second house as the substance of the figure — what they actually own, not what they aspire to. Planets here describe how money is made and what money is for. Venus or Jupiter in the second often blesses earning; Saturn there often slows it, sometimes building wealth that has waited a long time; Mars there spends what comes in and earns it back; the Moon there ties income to feeling.

## In a Chart
The second house ruler (the planet ruling the sign on the cusp) is the key indicator of livelihood. Its condition by sign, house, and aspect shows whether earning is easy or difficult, steady or volatile. The second house is also the house of values — what the figure refuses to sell, what they treat as sacred — and these often shape the work as much as the work shapes the bank account.

## Keywords
money, possessions, values, earning, the held, livelihood, the body's resources
