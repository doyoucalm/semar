# Study Hall (学堂)

## Header
**Category:** auspicious / scholarship. **Anchor:** day stem. **Rule:** the day stem looks up a single corresponding branch — always one of the elemental "long" positions of the day stem's element.

## Core Meaning
The star of the schoolroom. Study Hall represents the figure's natural environment for learning — the place, person, or institution that becomes their schoolhouse. Read as the figure who learns easily in formal settings and who is drawn to teaching when they have matured.

## In a Chart
Study Hall in the chart indicates the figure's relationship with formal learning. The star is especially fortunate for academic careers, teaching positions, and any work that involves transmission of skill. In combination with the Literary Star (文昌), Study Hall doubles the indication: the figure is suited both to learning (Study Hall) and to articulating what they have learned (Literary Star).

## When Activated
The star is activated when its branch appears in a luck pillar or annual transit. Such years often coincide with formal study, the start of a course, university enrolment, professional certification, or the assumption of a teaching role.

## Keywords
the schoolroom, formal learning, the academic environment, the teacher, the student, the lineage of knowledge
