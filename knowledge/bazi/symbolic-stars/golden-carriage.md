# Golden Carriage (金舆)

## Core Meaning
The star of comfort and noble support. Golden Carriage represents the figure's access to material comfort, particularly through marriage or partnership. Read as the support that arrives in a refined form — the spouse from a comfortable family, the partner whose resources lift the figure's life.

## In a Chart
A chart with the Golden Carriage often gives a figure who lives well — not always rich, but cared-for, accompanied, drawn by comfortable circumstances. The star is most strongly read on the day branch (which represents the spouse): a Golden Carriage on the day branch is one of the classical signs of a fortunate marriage. In other positions, it indicates support through family, work environment, or older children.

The classical metaphor: the figure is carried in a gilded carriage rather than walking. The carriage may be earned or it may be received as a gift; the star does not distinguish.

## When Activated
Golden Carriage activations in luck pillars or annual transits often coincide with marriage, the receipt of an inheritance, comfortable relocations, or the arrival of partnership-based support.

## Keywords
the comfort, the noble support, the fortunate partnership, the gilded carriage, marriage into ease, refined support
