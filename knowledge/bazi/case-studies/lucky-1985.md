**Day master:** 甲 (Yang Wood — the tall tree). Born in late spring (辰 month, earth taking over from wood). Hour: 丙 (Yang Fire) in 寅 (Tiger), the wood-tiger hour well before sunrise in the deep night.

Counting stems and main branch elements (surface + chief hidden stems):

- Wood: Year stem 乙 + Hour branch 寅 (甲 as main hidden stem) = **2 visible wood + day master**
- Fire: Hour stem 丙 + 寅 (丙 hidden inside) = **2 fire positions**

The chart is **earth-storage-heavy** with a **moderate-to-strong wood day master**. Compared to the erroneous 己巳 hour reading, the corrected chart carries notably more wood and fire: the day master has a live 寅 root in the hour, and 丙 fire (Eating God) lights the output pillar. Earth remains dominant in the career and marriage palaces, but the tree now stands on firmer footing.

## Day Master Strength

In classical analysis, 甲 born in late-spring 辰 month (earth month) is moderate: the earth environment is large, absorbing, and does not nourish wood naturally. The key supports are:

- **Year stem 乙 (Rob Wealth)**: yin-wood sibling — soft support, present but not firm.
- **Hour branch 寅 (main hidden stem 甲)**: the most significant corrected factor. 寅 is pure Yang Wood, the same nature as the day master. This gives 甲 a real, living root in the hour pillar. The tree is planted in fertile tiger-ground.
- **丙 hour stem (Eating God)**: fire drains wood slightly but represents the tree's productive flame — the day master giving, expressing, creating. A weak day master burdened by Eating God struggles; a moderate one with a wood root can handle it, and in fact thrives.

**Assessment:** The chart is **moderate day master, tilting toward sufficient**. It is neither weak enough to need heavy resource/companion support at all costs, nor strong enough to comfortably bear full force of the Seven Killings. The 庚 metal challenge remains serious but is now met by a more rooted tree.

## Ten Gods Around the Day Master

- **Year stem 乙:** Rob Wealth (劫财). The wood sibling — close ally, but yin and somewhat parasitic. Shares the day master's resources.
- **Year branch 丑:** holds 己 (Direct Wealth), 癸 (Direct Resource), 辛 (Direct Officer). Lineage vault: wealth, resource, and authority folded into the ancestral position.
- **Month stem 庚:** Seven Killings (七杀). The axe confronting the tree. The chart's central structural tension and the engine of discipline.
- **Month branch 辰:** holds 戊 (Indirect Wealth), 乙 (Rob Wealth), 癸 (Direct Resource). Career environment: indirect wealth and resource under a shared-wood surface.
- **Day branch 辰:** the marriage palace — same vault as month, recurring theme.
- **Hour stem 丙:** Eating God (食神). The day master's primary output star: creative expression, craft, teaching, art, food, language. The figure's most natural gift.
- **Hour branch 寅:** holds 甲 (Companion/比肩), 丙 (Eating God/食神), 戊 (Indirect Wealth/偏财). The hour pillar is a concentrated bundle of wood-fire-earth: identity, expression, and output all sitting together in the legacy position.

## Interactions

- **Two 辰 branches (month + day): self-punishment of 辰 (自刑)**. The doubled earth-vault in career and marriage positions creates internal friction between vocation and home — the same tension processed twice. The invitation is integration, not separation.
- **庚 + 乙: partial combination**. 庚 (Seven Killings) and 乙 (Rob Wealth) share the same chart — in certain year and luck pillar activations, the metal-wood tension takes on a interpersonal colour (a rival or partner drawing the figure's attention toward the same difficult relationships).
- **丑 + 辰 (month + year branches)**: two earth-storage vaults in proximity but not in direct classical interaction. They reinforce the theme of hidden resources waiting for activation.

## The Void (空亡) — Key Difference from the Old Reading

The **甲辰 旬 (decade group)** voids 寅 and 卯. With the corrected 丙寅 hour:

> **The hour branch 寅 falls in the void of the chart.**

This is one of the reading's most distinctive structural features. The hour pillar — representing late life, legacy, children, and the figure's creative output — rests on a branch that is emptied (空亡) relative to the day-pillar group. Classically, this suggests:

- The 食神's expression (Eating God in the hour stem) is prolific and genuine, but its material rewards may feel elusive or incomplete.
- Creative output reaches the world but the figure may not fully claim or experience its results.
- A spiritual or contemplative colouring to the late-life legacy: the output matters more for what it means than what it earns.
- 寅 itself is Yang Wood, directly supporting the day master — the void does not remove this support, but attenuates its solidity. The root is there; it fluctuates.

In the earlier (incorrect) 己巳 reading, the hour branch 巳 was **not** voided. The hour sat securely, with Direct Wealth as the legacy theme. With 丙寅, the output is more expressive and more spiritually weighted — and the worldly return is more uncertain.

## Shen Sha (Symbolic Stars) — Updated

- **Heavenly Noble (天乙贵人) for 甲:** 丑 and 未. Year branch 丑 still carries Heavenly Noble — lineage helpers remain in the chart.
- **Literary Star (文昌) for 甲:** 巳. **The 巳 branch is no longer in the chart** (it was the hour branch in the old reading). The Literary Star's branch is dormant — it may activate during luck pillars or annual branches that bring 巳 into the chart.
- **Golden Carriage (金舆) for 甲辰 day:** 辰. Both month and day branches are still 辰. The figure retains double comfort flowing through work and marriage.
- **Canopy (华盖) for 辰:** via the 申子辰 triad, the month and day 辰 branches carry Canopy — the contemplative, scholarly, artistic resonance persists.
- **Void (空亡) for 甲辰 旬:** 寅 and 卯. **寅 is now explicitly in the chart** (hour branch). The void in the output position is a live structural feature, not dormant.

## Reading

The corrected chart shows a Yang Wood tree with fire in its crown and tiger-wood at its roots, standing on a doubled field of earth with a metal axe at its side. The structure is more vital than the erroneous reading suggested: the day master is not fragile, and the Eating God (creative expression) in the hour speaks clearly as the figure's primary mode of operating in the world.

The Seven Killings at 庚 still constitutes the chart's central challenge — the discipline, the external adversary, the structure that the tree must negotiate. But the tree is now more substantially rooted (寅 branch), and the output (食神) can channel some of the metal-wood tension into productive work rather than pure resistance.

The doubled 辰 self-punishment remains the recurring structural theme: career and home carry the same earth-vault signature, and the figure is asked to reconcile vocation and household as a single integrated practice rather than two competing demands.

The Eating God in the voided hour pillar gives the reading its most distinctive quality: a figure whose creative gifts are genuine and evident, whose teaching or art or expression resonates — but who may feel that the full fruit of that expression slips slightly out of reach, or belongs to others more than to himself. The late-life legacy is real; its material weight is lighter than its inner weight.

The corrected reading is more contemplative and expressive-first; the erroneous reading was more wealth- and discipline-first. The structural skeleton (earth-heavy, Seven Killings dominant, doubled 辰 self-punishment) is unchanged.

## Lessons for Reading

- **Birth-time accuracy is non-negotiable for the hour pillar.** A 6-hour error completely rotates the output star, changes void relationships, and shifts the day master's assessed strength. All subsequent luck-pillar analysis and transit interpretation must rebuild from the correct hour.
- **Void in the hour pillar is a signature**, not a curse. The creative output is real; the chart asks the figure to hold his legacy lightly — to give it without demanding its return.
- **食神 (Eating God) as the sole output star** (without the 伤官 Hurting Officer) is a softer, more sustaining energy than a 伤官-heavy chart. The figure expresses without rebellion; the gift is offered more than asserted.
- **Repeated 辰 in career + marriage**: a chart that processes the same integration question in two of its most-read positions. The figure is not asked to choose between work and home but to keep discovering how they are the same work.
