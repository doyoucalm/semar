# Three of Cups

## Image
Three women dance in a circle, each raising a cup. Their robes are coloured for harvest. A pumpkin and grapes lie at their feet.

## Core Meaning
Celebration in community. The bond of two has expanded to three or more — the festival, the gathering, the toast among friends. Read as joy with others, communal life, the recognised abundance.

## Upright
Tradition reads it as the wedding party, the birth celebration, the reunion of old friends, the chosen family at a feast. The three cups in the air are equal; this is not the hierarchy of the throne but the circle of peers.

## Reversed
The gathering soured — gossip rather than joy, the celebration that masks something — or the figure left out of the circle. Sometimes too much of a good thing: revelry that has lost touch with the occasion.

## Keywords
celebration, community, the chosen family, joy with others, the toast, gossip (reversed), exclusion (reversed)
