# Ace of Cups

## Image
A hand from a cloud holds a chalice overflowing with five streams of water. A dove descends into the cup carrying a wafer. The cup is inscribed with an M or inverted W; lotuses float on the pool below.

## Core Meaning
The cup of grace. A new feeling, a new relationship, an opening of the heart, the inrush of love or intuition. Read as the source-water of the emotional life beginning to flow.

## Upright
Tradition reads it as the offering of love (romantic, parental, spiritual), the breakthrough of feeling after long dryness, the first stirring of a creative or devotional life. The cup is full — receiving is the question.

## Reversed
The cup blocked, spilled, or refused. The feeling is present but cannot land — defended heart, emotion that cannot be received, source dammed at the well.

## Keywords
grace, opening of heart, new feeling, love offered, source, the cup overflowing, blocked feeling (reversed)
