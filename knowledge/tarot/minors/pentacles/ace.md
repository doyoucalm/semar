# Ace of Pentacles

## Image
A hand from a cloud holds a single large pentacle over a garden — flowers below, a hedge-tunnel leading to mountains beyond. Lilies and roses bloom; a path runs through the hedge.

## Core Meaning
The gift of earth. A material beginning — an opportunity, a sum of money, a new job, a new piece of work that has substance. Read as the seed of livelihood, the offering of ground.

## Upright
Tradition reads it as the inheritance, the bonus, the loan that funds the venture, the contract signed, the job offered. The path through the hedge points to a longer journey — this is the beginning of a road, not a windfall to be spent.

## Reversed
The opportunity refused, or the gift wasted. Sometimes the financial start that does not arrive on the promised date; sometimes the material gain that is treated as the whole answer when it is in fact the start of a question.

## Keywords
material beginning, the gift of earth, opportunity, the seed of livelihood, the contract, the bonus, wasted gift (reversed)
