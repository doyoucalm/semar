# Six Clashes (六冲)

## Header
**Type:** branch opposition. **Pairs:** 子午 / 丑未 / 寅申 / 卯酉 / 辰戌 / 巳亥. **Effect:** mutual collision; the two branches strike against each other and destabilise both.

## Core Meaning
The pairs that strike. The Six Clashes are the twelve branches arranged as six pairs of opposites — each pair separated by 180° on the branch circle, the way Aries and Libra are opposite in the zodiac. Read as the friction points in the chart, where two parts of life refuse to align.

## The Six Pairs

| Pair | Element clash |
| --- | --- |
| 子 ↔ 午 | Water ↔ Fire |
| 丑 ↔ 未 | Earth ↔ Earth (different storage seasons) |
| 寅 ↔ 申 | Wood ↔ Metal |
| 卯 ↔ 酉 | Wood ↔ Metal |
| 辰 ↔ 戌 | Earth ↔ Earth (different storage seasons) |
| 巳 ↔ 亥 | Fire ↔ Water |

The element pairs are the controlling cycle: water clashes fire, metal clashes wood, earth-clashes-earth is the storage clash (the two earth branches hold different elements internally and contest each other).

## In a Chart
A clash between branches is a friction point. Tradition reads clashes as places where the figure encounters movement — change of address (year-day clash), trouble between parents (year-month clash), restlessness in marriage (day-month or day-hour clash). The clash between day and year is read with special weight, since it implies tension between the figure and their origins.

## When the Clash Helps
A clash is welcome when it opens a stuck place. A chart full of earth with a 辰戌 clash may benefit from the friction — the storage is broken open and the hidden stems are released. Clashes can also activate beneficial hidden elements: 寅 holds wood, fire, and earth; when 寅 is clashed by 申, those hidden elements come out of storage.

## When the Clash Hurts
A clash is unwelcome when it strikes a useful branch. A useful 卯 (the chart's important wood root) clashed by 酉 weakens or destroys that root, and the figure may lose what the 卯 represents. Clashes in luck pillars or annual transits often coincide with sudden changes: relocation, ending a relationship, leaving a job.

## Keywords
the clash, the friction, the opening of storage, the destruction of root, sudden change, restlessness
