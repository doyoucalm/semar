## Citation Conventions

The page-level Sources sections at the end of each knowledge-base entry give:
- Author or compiler name.
- Italicised title.
- Publisher, place, and year of edition consulted.
- Page references where directly relevant.

Where the knowledge-base citation says only "*The Pictorial Key to the Tarot*" without further detail, the full citation is here in the bibliography.

For the I Ching and numerology subsections (the smaller traditions in this codex), canonical references are listed inline in the relevant knowledge-base pages — there is no separate bibliography file yet. When the I Ching and numerology research deepens, separate bibliography files will be added here.
