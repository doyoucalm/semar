# Page of Cups

## Image
A young figure in a flowery tunic stands on a beach, holding up a cup. A fish leans out of the cup, looking at him. He looks back, amused and unalarmed.

## Core Meaning
The dreamer. The Page of Cups carries unexpected emotional or intuitive news — a feeling from the unconscious, a creative impulse, a vulnerable confession. Read as the sensitive young one, the artistic beginning, the message from the heart.

## Upright
Tradition reads it as the artistic apprentice, the gentle child, the lover whose vulnerability is not yet defended, or the dream that brings a piece of guidance. The fish in the cup is the unconscious's small offering — strange and welcome.

## Reversed
Sensitivity becomes overwhelm, or the dream is mistaken for a plan. Sometimes the Page reversed is the figure who cannot tell their own feelings from someone else's.

## Keywords
the dreamer, artistic apprentice, sensitive, the fish in the cup, message of feeling, overwhelm (reversed)
