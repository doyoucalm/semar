# Triangular Combinations (三合 / 三会)

## Header
**Type:** branch triad combinations. **Two flavours:**
- **Three Harmonies (三合):** three branches forming a strong element triad — 申子辰 (water), 亥卯未 (wood), 寅午戌 (fire), 巳酉丑 (metal).
- **Directional Triads (三会 / 方局):** three branches of the same season — 寅卯辰 (spring/wood), 巳午未 (summer/fire), 申酉戌 (autumn/metal), 亥子丑 (winter/water).

## Core Meaning
The triple forming a season or a current. Where two-branch combinations soften a pair, the triangular combinations rally three branches around a single elemental theme. Read as the chart's major elemental currents — the directions in which its energy concentrates.

## Three Harmonies (三合)
Each triad combines the long, peak, and storage branches of an element into a strong elemental concentration. **申子辰** is the water triangle: 申 (water's long), 子 (water's peak), 辰 (water's storage). The same logic builds 亥卯未 (wood), 寅午戌 (fire), and 巳酉丑 (metal). When all three branches appear in the chart, the elemental signature is unmistakable and often dominates.

Partial harmonies — two of the three branches — still combine, but with reduced intensity. A "half-combination" requires the peak branch (子 for water, 卯 for wood, 午 for fire, 酉 for metal). 申辰 without 子 is weaker than 子辰 or 申子.

## Directional Triads (三会)
The three branches of the same season — 寅卯辰 spring, 巳午未 summer, 申酉戌 autumn, 亥子丑 winter — together form a "direction." The directional combination is even stronger than the Three Harmonies because it represents an unmixed seasonal current. A chart with 寅卯辰 in three branches is overwhelmingly wood in the spring sense.

## In a Chart
Triangular combinations reshape the element balance of the chart. A weak fire 丁 day master with 寅午戌 in the chart is no longer weak — the fire triangle has lifted the fire to dominant. Triangles also override clashes within them: 寅午戌 ignores the 寅申 or 午子 clashes that other branches might pose, because the triangle is committed.

## When the Triangle Helps
The triangle helps when the chart needs the element it forms. A 甲 wood day master with the 亥卯未 wood triangle has its day-master massively reinforced. A water triangle in a chart that needs water for its day master is similar.

## When the Triangle Hurts
The triangle hurts when it forms the unwelcome element. A weak 丁 fire facing 申子辰 (water triangle) is drowned — the water overwhelms the fire. The remedy is the controlling element: earth to dam the water, fire to push back, or wood to drain the water by being fed.

## Keywords
the three harmonies, the directional triad, the elemental current, the season formed, the dominant signature, the reshaping of the chart
