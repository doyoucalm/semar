# The Sixth House

## Header
**Cadent.** **Natural sign:** Virgo. **Natural ruler:** Mercury. **Domain:** daily work, health, service, routine, small animals, employees.

## Core Meaning
The room of the daily craft. The sixth house holds the work that is not the public career but the daily repetition — the trade, the practice, the health regimen, the small obligations that make a life function. Read as the figure's relationship with body, work, and the daily acts of maintenance.

## Upright
Tradition reads the sixth as the house of illness, service, and toil — and also of skill, craft, and the daily work that supports a real life. Planets here describe the texture of work and the patterns of health. Mercury or Mars here often gives strong work capacity; Saturn here gives a craftsperson who works long and slow, sometimes with chronic body complaints. Hellenistic sources call this house "Bad Fortune" — it can be the house of difficulty as well as discipline.

## In a Chart
The sixth house ruler's condition shows the quality of the daily work life and the relationship to body. The sixth-twelfth axis is the service-dissolution axis: where service is given (sixth) and where the self dissolves into something larger (twelfth). Modern practice often reads the sixth as where the chart learns the discipline of devotion to the small task — the doctor's rounds, the writer's pages, the trainer's reps.

## Keywords
daily work, the body, health, service, craft, routine, the small task, the daily devotion
