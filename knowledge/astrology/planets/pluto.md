# Pluto (♇)

## Glyph
A circle on a crescent on a cross, or the PL monogram (for Percival Lowell, the planet's predicted hunter). The underworld god's tipped head.

## Header
**Modern ruler of:** Scorpio. **Discovered:** 1930 by Clyde Tombaugh. **Orbital period:** ~248 years (highly elliptical). **Mythic figure:** Pluto / Hades, lord of the underworld; Persephone returning; the phoenix consumed and rising.

## Core Meaning
The function of irreversible transformation. Pluto is the principle by which what cannot continue is taken apart and what must continue is forged again under pressure. Read as death and rebirth, power, the buried — the part of the figure that meets the underworld and comes back changed.

## Upright
Modern tradition reads Pluto as the depth psychologist, the surgeon of the soul, the figure willing to descend. The house holding Pluto describes where the chart undergoes its great compressions and emerges with the kind of authority that has been paid for. Aspects to personal planets describe what is taken into the underworld: Pluto to the Sun rebuilds the identity, often through crisis; Pluto to the Moon transforms the emotional body, sometimes through inherited family material; Pluto to Venus forges loves of the kind that change the lover.

## Afflicted
The same depth turned to compulsion. A Pluto in hard aspect, especially when other supporting structures are weak, may indicate a figure who confuses intensity with intimacy, who uses power to fill the wound power made, who cannot let anything end cleanly. The shadow Pluto manipulates, controls, and obsesses.

## In a Chart
Pluto's house is the chart's great compost — where decay becomes the soil of new growth, where what is hidden eventually surfaces. Generational by sign (each Pluto sign lasts 12–32 years and marks a cohort), Pluto becomes personal through house and aspect. The Pluto square to natal Pluto (~age 36–45 for most modern births) is read as the underworld passage of the second half of life.

## Keywords
transformation, death and rebirth, the underworld, power, the depths, the buried, compulsion (shadow), manipulation (shadow), obsession (shadow)
