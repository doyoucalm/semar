# The Fourth House

## Header
**Cusp:** the IC (Imum Coeli — the lowest point of the chart). **Angular.** **Natural sign:** Cancer. **Natural ruler:** Moon. **Domain:** the home, the family of origin, the father (in traditional reading) or the lineage, the deep roots, the private interior.

## Core Meaning
The root of the chart. The fourth house begins at the lowest point of the sky at the moment of birth — the place beneath the earth, the foundation. Read as where the figure comes from and what they return to at the end of the day: the home, the family lineage, the inner sanctuary.

## Upright
Tradition reads the fourth as the house of the father, the homeland, the place of birth, and (in older sources) the conditions of one's death. Modern practice reads it as the family of origin, the deep emotional foundation, the private life. Planets here describe what is in the basement of the chart — what was inherited, what is hidden under the floorboards, what gives the figure their sense of belonging.

## In a Chart
The fourth house ruler's condition shows the quality of the inner life and the figure's relationship to roots. The Moon's condition is often read in parallel, since the Moon naturally governs home. The fourth and tenth houses form an axis: where the fourth holds the private foundation, the tenth holds the public face. A figure cannot stand tall in the tenth without ground in the fourth.

## Keywords
the home, the lineage, the family of origin, the foundation, the IC, the private life, the deep root
