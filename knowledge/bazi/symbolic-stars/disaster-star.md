# Disaster Star (灾煞)

## Header
**Category:** inauspicious / sudden trouble. **Anchor:** year or day branch. **Rule:** the year (or day) branch determines a single branch — always the "peak" of the triad opposite the home one — that, if present elsewhere in the chart, brings the Disaster Star.

## Lookup
| Year/Day branch | Disaster Star |
| --- | --- |
| 申, 子, 辰 | 午 |
| 亥, 卯, 未 | 酉 |
| 寅, 午, 戌 | 子 |
| 巳, 酉, 丑 | 卯 |

The Disaster Star is the peak branch of the triad that clashes the home triad — the opposite peak.

## Core Meaning
The star of sudden misfortune. Disaster Star is read as accidents, illnesses that come without warning, sudden reversals — the harder counterpart of the Robbery Star, which targets material loss. Read as the unexpected event that strikes from outside, the kind of trouble that cannot be planned away.

## In a Chart
A chart with the Disaster Star is read carefully. The star's house position colours the domain affected: in the year branch, family-related troubles; in the month branch, work-environment events; in the day branch, in close relationships or in the body; in the hour branch, with children or in later years.

Tradition is severe about this star, but modern practice tempers the reading — the star is a hint of fragility in the domain it sits, not a sentence of doom. Many charts hold the Disaster Star and reach long, satisfying lives; the star is a counsel toward care, not a verdict.

## When Activated
Activation by luck pillar or transit can coincide with sudden events — health scares, accidents, sudden departures. Activation is intensified when the Disaster Star is also involved in a clash or punishment.

## Keywords
sudden trouble, the accident, the unexpected reversal, fragility, health caution, the strike from outside
