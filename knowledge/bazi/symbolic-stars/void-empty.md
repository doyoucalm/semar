# Void / Empty (空亡)

## Header
**Category:** mixed (often inauspicious, sometimes transformative). **Anchor:** the day pillar's 旬 (xún), the ten-day cycle to which the day pillar belongs. **Rule:** within the 60-pillar sexagenary cycle, each group of 10 consecutive pillars (one 旬) leaves out two of the twelve branches. Those two branches are the void branches for that 旬.

## The Six Xún and Their Voids

| 旬 (starting pillar) | Void branches |
| --- | --- |
| 甲子 旬 (甲子–癸酉) | 戌, 亥 |
| 甲戌 旬 (甲戌–癸未) | 申, 酉 |
| 甲申 旬 (甲申–癸巳) | 午, 未 |
| 甲午 旬 (甲午–癸卯) | 辰, 巳 |
| 甲辰 旬 (甲辰–癸丑) | 寅, 卯 |
| 甲寅 旬 (甲寅–癸亥) | 子, 丑 |

Lucky's day pillar 甲辰 belongs to the 甲辰 旬, so 寅 and 卯 are void in his chart.

## Core Meaning
The branch that does not fully count. Voids represent positions where the branch's expected function is absent or hollow — the office without an officer, the seat without a person. Read as themes that drift in and out, that promise without delivering, that appear important but are difficult to grasp.

## In a Chart
A void branch in the chart is read as the function being weakened or hollowed. The branch is still present, but it does not act with full force; it may even invert. Classical readings of void positions:
- **Void in year branch:** weakened tie to lineage or origin; the figure may feel less rooted in family identity.
- **Void in month branch:** unstable career environment, the work that promises but disappoints.
- **Void in day branch:** marriage or close partnership feels less anchored than its outward form suggests.
- **Void in hour branch:** unclear or fragile relationship with children, or a hollow legacy phase.

## When Void Is Transformative
Some classical sources read voids as creating space for spiritual or transformative work — the empty seat into which something new can come. A figure with a void in their chart may find that the void position becomes the site of the chart's deepest unconventional gifts: artists, mystics, scholars often have meaningful voids.

## When Activated
The void can be temporarily "filled" — a clashing branch from a luck pillar or transit may activate the void and bring its themes briefly into focus, often as a sudden event whose effect fades quickly.

## Keywords
the empty seat, the hollow branch, the absent officer, the drift, the appearance without substance, the transformative emptiness
