# Ace of Wands

## Image
A hand emerges from a cloud, holding a budding wooden staff. Leaves grow from it; eight yods fall away. Below, a fertile landscape with a distant castle on a hill.

## Core Meaning
The spark of beginning — a new fire, a new venture, the moment a creative or vocational impulse declares itself. Read as the seed of action.

## Upright
This is the suit's first card and the cleanest expression of its element. Tradition reads it as inspiration, a creative breakthrough, the beginning of an enterprise, the surge of confident energy that opens a chapter. The budding leaves on the staff matter: this is alive, not a dry tool.

## Reversed
The spark misfires — a beginning delayed, an inspiration that does not land, the project announced before its energy is real. Sometimes a creative block.

## Keywords
spark, beginning, inspiration, new venture, creative impulse, the lit torch
