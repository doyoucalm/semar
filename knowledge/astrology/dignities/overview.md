# Essential Dignities

## Header
**Five traditional dignities:** domicile (rulership), exaltation, triplicity, term, face. **Two debilities:** detriment (opposite of domicile), fall (opposite of exaltation). Modern practice often uses only the two strongest: domicile and exaltation, with detriment and fall as the parallel debilities.

## Core Meaning
The signs in which planets are strong or weak. Dignity is the system that tells how well a planet can do its work in a given sign. A planet in its domicile is at home — it can act fluently. A planet in detriment is in a house belonging to its opposite — it has to operate against the local language. The same logic applies to exaltation (the sign that lifts the planet) and fall (the sign that depresses it).

## Domicile
Each of the seven traditional planets rules two signs (except the Sun and Moon, which rule one each). A planet in its domicile is at full strength, able to express its principle without translation.

| Planet | Domicile |
| --- | --- |
| Sun | Leo |
| Moon | Cancer |
| Mercury | Gemini, Virgo |
| Venus | Taurus, Libra |
| Mars | Aries, Scorpio |
| Jupiter | Sagittarius, Pisces |
| Saturn | Capricorn, Aquarius |

Modern practice assigns Uranus to Aquarius, Neptune to Pisces, and Pluto to Scorpio as co-rulers. The traditional rulerships still apply alongside the modern.

## Exaltation
Each planet has one sign of exaltation, where its principle is honoured even more highly than at home in some respects.

| Planet | Exaltation | Degree |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sun | Aries | 19° |
| Moon | Taurus | 3° |
| Mercury | Virgo | 15° |
| Venus | Pisces | 27° |
| Mars | Capricorn | 28° |
| Jupiter | Cancer | 15° |
| Saturn | Libra | 21° |

The exact exaltation degree gives the strongest expression; the whole sign carries the dignity at lesser strength.

## Detriment and Fall
A planet in detriment is in the sign opposite its domicile; in fall, opposite its exaltation. The planet is weakened — it can still function, but the local soil resists it. Mars in Libra (detriment) finds direct action awkward; the Sun in Libra (fall) finds individual identity diluted by the need for the other; the Moon in Capricorn (detriment) finds the emotional body expected to operate as if it were structure; Saturn in Aries (fall) finds patient structure overrun by initiative.

## In a Chart
Dignity is a quick reading of how much help or resistance a planet has from its sign. A dignified malefic (Mars in Capricorn, Saturn in Libra) can be a great strength in the chart. An afflicted benefic (Venus in Scorpio, Jupiter in Capricorn) can be a real difficulty. Tradition combined essential dignity with accidental dignity (house position, angularity, aspect to lights) to produce a full strength estimate.

## Keywords
domicile, exaltation, detriment, fall, the dignified planet, the debilitated planet, essential dignity, accidental dignity
