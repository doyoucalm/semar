# Literary Star (文昌贵人 / Star of Arts)

## Header
**Category:** auspicious / scholarship. **Anchor:** day stem. **Rule:** the day stem looks up a single corresponding branch.

The Literary Star branch is always the "eating god" or "hurting officer" of the day stem in the Ten Gods system — the branch that drains the day master into expression and intellect.

## Core Meaning
The star of scholarship, writing, and refined expression. The Literary Star represents the figure's capacity for study, language, and artistic articulation. Read as the gift for words, the love of books, the figure whose mind is shaped by what they read.

## In a Chart
A chart with the Literary Star tends to have intellectual interests, often expressed through writing, teaching, or careful study. The star is especially favoured when in the day or hour branch — the figure's own expression and outputs carry literary or scholarly quality.

Tradition reads multiple Literary Stars as a strong sign of a writer, teacher, or scholar. The star is also read as a fortunate position for exam-taking and academic achievement — a chart with the Literary Star activated by transit in an exam year is read favourably.

## Related Star: Study Hall (学堂)
A closely related star, the Study Hall, follows a similar lookup rule and indicates the figure's relationship with formal learning environments. The two stars together — Literary Star plus Study Hall — strengthen the scholarly reading.

## When Activated
The star is activated when its branch appears in a luck pillar or annual year. Such years often coincide with the figure publishing, completing a degree, receiving recognition for written or intellectual work, or finding their voice in a new domain.

## Keywords
the writer, the scholar, refined expression, books, study, articulate language, the academic gift
